What do the people need?

Overview

The course tasked my group with coming up with solutionsto an existing issue. Buffer was an idea we formed on the idea of community, of how people will always need a shared space in which they are able to express themselves. Through trial and error, and listening to potential consumers Buffer was able to maintain its roots, and serve the community.

Learn more

Our group contained many members from all walks of life, which is how we were able to come up with such a varied taste of ideas. What we all came to find out was that we shared a strong passion with movie/tv show watching.

Our proto persona came about after asking ourselves who our movie app would be for. We came tothe consensus that this app would most likely attract people who are already deeply invested into the medium

We looked at other apps that were already on the market that had similar ideas as Buffer. We found that while many of the other apps were highly specialized towards cataloging or recommendations, very few dared to combine the two. And even fewer had the notion of creating a watch party forum. While I presented the idea that the app be used as a means to keep things better organized cross streaming platforms. Other members of my group are truly to thank for coming up with the ideas towards including watch parties.

How do we help?

The issue that we were finding with our users was that they felt as if they had less of a third space to connect with their friends. Which caused distance and isolation issues. Savannah, one of our team members was able to gather this great quote during her research of Dr. Laurie Santos "Sharing a good experience deepens enjoyment of the moment." And that's what Buffer at its core wishes to create. A good experience.
